    We've been running this charity since early September, 2018. Oggi teaches coding to people looking to get into web development. With any kind of teaching it is bound to be a struggle at times, so we here at Oggi's BeerFundMe look to raise money to make sure he can have a cold one to relax after class. As many people know one of the keys to a successful life is to be able to unwind after a long day, you could make that happen for Oggi when you donate today! Look at our contact page to put your donation in!
